Wake Up Manila 2018 - Curated Job Convention by Springboard

Wake Up Manila! 2018 is the biggest job convention in the Philippine market today, and it's organised by one of the top headhunters in the region - Springboard Philippines. We've been hard at work and now we're partnering with a startup called Tambio to help the Contact Center Association of the Philippines - CCAP, IT & Business Process Management of the Philippines - IBPAP, & Philippine startup companies generate and maintain their talent as the job market evolves. We bring you Wake Up Manila! 2018 - the most sought after job convention this 2018, slated this July 6-7 at SMX Convention Centre. We are expecting 2,000 - 4,000 attendees - from fresh grads, to interns, to IT professionals, customer experience personnel, young professionals and everything in between.
